Dr. Wang now is a researcher at WeChat Pay. He received his PhD degree in computer science from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, and his BSc, MSc degrees in computer science from Northwestern Polytechnical University. His research interests mainly include information diffusion modeling, graph learning and recommendation. Dr. Wang has published 20+ papers in prestigious venues, including TKDE, TNNLS, TKDD, TAC, SIGIR, IJCAI, AAAI, UbiComp, CIKM, COLING, etc.
